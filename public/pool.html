IPL Playoff Pool Competition

What is this?

It is time to get hyped up for IPL. To make this year's season even more exciting, I am proposing a fun competition pool to predict the 4 playoff teams in this year’s IPL.

What's in it for me?

Every player will pledge 5 dollars to participate in the competition and the winner gets all the money plus bragging rights on his/her buddies forever.

Probability says you have a 1 in 70 chance to get it right, but you could make it a lot closer with your smarts!!

I'm in. What should I do now?

All you need to do is rank the 8 teams based on your confidence in them qualifying for playoffs. The points of the 4 qualified teams will be added for each player and the player with the most points wins the pool.

E.g.:

If I pick them this way,


8-Hyderabad Heroes

7-Ahmedabad Rockets

6-Royal Bengal Tigers

5 -Chandigarh Lions

4-Lahore Badshahs

3 -Mumbai Champs

2 -Chennai Superstars

1 -Delhi Giants

And if Hyderabad Heroes, Chandigarh Lions, Lahore Badshahs, and Delhi Giants get qualified, I would get 8+5+4+1 = 18 points.

In the case of a tie, the player with the most points of the finalist teams will win the pool.

If the tie is not broken, the player with the most points of the champion team will win the pool.

if it's still a tie, the tied players will share the money.

It's a long competition and there could be a lot of twists and turns on the road to playoffs, let’s see who the winner would be on Nov 3rd.
